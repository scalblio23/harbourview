## Selected Approach: Swiss Modernism meets Financial Clarity

Chosen for its balance of trust, clarity, and warmth. The card-based layout keeps users focused, the navy/amber palette signals financial authority, and the horizontal slide transitions create a sense of forward momentum without distraction.
